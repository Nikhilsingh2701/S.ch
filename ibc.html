Insolvency and Bankruptcy¬†Code

Our firm offers expert advisory and compliance services under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), assisting stakeholders through the complexities of corporate insolvency and resolution processes. We provide end-to-end support to creditors, corporate debtors, insolvency professionals, and resolution applicants by helping navigate legal, financial, and regulatory challenges efficiently. Our services include preparation and filing of insolvency applications before the NCLT, assisting in claims verification, forensic audits, resolution plan evaluation, and drafting of restructuring proposals. We also advise on liquidation processes, asset valuation, and compliance with statutory timelines and reporting requirements. With a deep understanding of IBC provisions, allied laws, and sector-specific intricacies, we ensure that our clients receive strategic, compliant, and outcome-driven solutions. Whether you are seeking to recover dues, restructure distressed assets, or participate in a resolution process, our dedicated team ensures timely execution, transparency, and alignment with your business interests.
