Registrar of Company

We offer a full spectrum of services related to compliance with the Registrar of Companies (ROC) under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). Our team assists businesses in meeting their statutory obligations right from company incorporation and obtaining necessary registrations to regular compliance filings and event-based reporting. Our ROC services include drafting and filing of incorporation documents, annual returns (AOC-4, MGT-7), Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC), maintenance of statutory registers, and handling changes in directorship, capital structure, or registered office. We also provide advisory on corporate restructuring, conversion of company types (e.g., private to public), and striking off defunct companies. In case of non-compliance or regulatory notices, we offer expert representation and rectification support before ROC authorities. By ensuring timely and accurate filings, we help clients avoid penalties and maintain good legal standing.
